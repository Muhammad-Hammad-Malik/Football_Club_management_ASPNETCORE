About Club

FC Bayern has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1900. The club has won numerous domestic and international titles, including 31 Bundesliga titles, 20 DFB-Pokal cups, and 6 UEFA Champions League titles.

Mission statement

"Our mission at Bayern Club is to promote excellence in football, foster a strong sense of community among our supporters, and inspire the next generation of athletes to achieve their full potential. We strive to uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship and integrity, both on and off the field, and to create a culture of inclusivity and diversity that celebrates the unique talents and backgrounds of all our players, staff, and fans. Through our commitment to excellence, teamwork, and respect, we aim to be a shining example of what a football club can achieve when it is driven by passion, dedication, and a deep love of the game."
